Becoming still

It took a lot for me to even begin something like this. There were moments where I convinced myself it wasn’t worth it, that it wouldn’t be “good enough” to matter. But I’ve come to realise that waiting for perfect conditions is just another form of avoidance. So I started where I was, with the skills I had, and built something small that speaks louder than silence. This site is personal — not in the way that tells you everything about me, but in the way that shows you I’m trying. Trying to move forward. Trying to make sense of things. Trying to create something that reflects both who I am now and who I’m becoming.
It’s easy to feel invisible when your path hasn’t been straightforward. But in building this, I’ve found a kind of visibility that doesn’t rely on achievements alone — one rooted in honesty, effort, and the quiet discipline of showing up. I don’t know where exactly this road will lead, but I’m no longer afraid to walk it. If this site stands for anything, it’s that beginnings don’t need permission. They just need action. And this — this is mine.
